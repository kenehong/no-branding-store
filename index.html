Note Bible

Read scripture. Have a thought. Write it down. Done.

Screenshot
Video
Why this exists

Every Bible app adds study tools, cross-references, reading plans, social sharing. None of them just let you write a note. This app does one thing: captures your thought right after you read. No account. No sync. No features.

Scope

It does

  • Write a note
  • Attach to a verse
  • Search your notes
  • Work offline

It doesn't

  • Accounts
  • Social features
  • Reading plans
  • Study tools
  • Push notifications

Weather Outfit

Check the weather. See what to wear. Go outside.

Why this exists

Some kids can't read temperature numbers. "54°F" means nothing to them. This app shows a character wearing the right clothes — t-shirt when it's hot, puffer coat when it's cold, raincoat when it rains. One glance, done. Designed for tablets and smart displays (Google Nest Hub, Echo Show). Three sensory modes for kids who need less visual stimulation.

Scope

It does

  • Show outfit by temperature
  • Walk animation characters
  • Weather effects (rain, snow, sun)
  • 3 sensory modes (Standard / Calm / Still)
  • Location-based weather

It doesn't

  • Accounts
  • Hourly forecasts
  • Weather alerts
  • Multiple locations
  • Sound effects

Headsup

What you don't know can ruin your trip. We find the blind spots so you don't have to.

Video
Why this exists

You booked the flight. You picked the hotel. But nobody told you there's no cell service in the national park, or that a wildfire closed the road you planned to drive. Travel apps help you book — none of them tell you what you don't know to search for. Headsup finds those blind spots before your trip does.

Scope

It does

  • Multi-leg trip builder
  • Must-know alerts (wildfires, closures)
  • Road conditions and route info
  • What to bring (visa, adapter, gear)
  • Safety map by neighborhood
  • Local transport recommendations
  • Weather with daily breakdown

It doesn't

  • Book flights or hotels
  • Accounts
  • Itinerary planning
  • Social features
  • Price comparison
